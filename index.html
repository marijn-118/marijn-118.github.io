SF Crime: Two Decades of Data

Welcome to my page analyzing crime trends in San Francisco. I will show a few vizualiazations that I created to explore the data. The visualizations include a static plot of yearly crime trends, an interactive Plotly chart of hourly crime patterns, and a Folium heatmap showing geospatial hotspots. Each visualization is accompanied by an explanation of the key insights and findings.

Yearly Crime Trends

Large dips for several crimes during covid (2020) such as theft and assault. Burglaries showed a initial increase in 2020.

Hourly Crime Patterns

This interactive Plotly chart shows the distribution of crimes across different hours of the day. Click on the legend to isolate specific crime types and observe their temporal patterns.

Geospatial Hotspots

This Folium heatmap visualizes the geographic distribution of crimes in San Francisco. The feedback loop of policing can be seen in the clustering of incidents in certain neighborhoods, which may influence resource allocation and patrol strategies.
